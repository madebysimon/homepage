In highly effective and team-oriented environments interrupting an established system breaks routines of everybody involved.
Since it is in our nature to resist change, people have to be prepared, gradually and individually guided along the way. A single reactant individual has the potential to tear down motivation of the whole team and deem your project to failure.
If you want your team to be able to focus on the transition, you'll need someone unbiased to address these factors, serve the team in the name of the project and manage the heat.

Why this site?
While it is a little overkill for now, maybe I'll grow into it.
I most certainly enjoyed the process of building it as I'm really into webdesign / UX and wanted to bring one of my creations to life.
After all what is the use to design if it only sits in a drawer and has no audience.
Why open source?
I believe in synergy and collective benefit. Put your work out there and thanks to the internet you might make someones day.
Yet I also believe in intellectual property and fair use.
